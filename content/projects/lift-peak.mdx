## Introduction

Lift Peak is a community-driven app designed for gym-goers who want more than just a workout tracker. It bridges the gap between fitness tracking, social interaction, and personalized progress analysis, making the gym experience engaging and motivating. Whether you're a beginner needing guidance, an enthusiast aiming to break through plateaus, or a coach managing clients, Lift Peak offers tools and a supportive community to help users of all levels stay connected, track their goals, and continuously improve.
