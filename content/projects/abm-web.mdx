## Challenge

Ace Battle Association was very specific client, since it is a new organization,
without any previous IT projects implemented. Throughout a year working for this
companies we changed multiple projects and still updating existed.

<MdxImage
  src="https://storage.googleapis.com/abe_cloud_storage/website%2Fsportsmens.png"
  width={800}
  className="mx-auto mt-5 mb-4"
  height={500}
/>

The main features is catigorized in three groups:
Informational platform
Members portal
Event landing pages and registrations

The platform includes results monitoring, runner profiles and portal, news, articles, rules explanation as a informational platform.

There is a memberships in organization which includes possibility to find a team, register and participate in events, multiple roles as subscription plans etc.

The ogranization is able to announce competition, the landing page appears on the website. They can control the accessibility and registrations for events.
